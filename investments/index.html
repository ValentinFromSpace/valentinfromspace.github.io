Crypto Portfolio

My goal concerning this portfolio was to 1) concentrate rather than diversify, 2) cover multiple distinct industries and 3) identify the most fundamental and forward-thinking protocols led by confident and focused teams.

  • Cardano (ADA)
    Cardano is a verifiably secure and decentralized proof-of-stake blockchain with native multi-asset and smart contract capabilities. The project has a strong focus on Africa.
  • Filecoin (FIL)
    Filecoin is a decentrlized storage network with its own proof-of-spacetime consensus mechanism. Development is led by Protocol Labs, the creators of IPFS and IPLD, the next-generation internet protocols.
  • Energy Web (EWT)
    Providing an energy operating system for decentralized energy grids to large partners.
  • Ultra.io (UOS)
    Starting as a game store based on NFTs and incentives for buyers, developers and content creators, with plans to become an all-in-one entertainment platform connected through an underlying blockchain
  • Chainlink (LINK)
    An oracle, providing data to smart contracts on any chain.
