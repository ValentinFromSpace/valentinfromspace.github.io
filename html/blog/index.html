Movies

18 Feb 2019

Each film in this list tells of the struggle and reward of an epic journey. They feature an epic world to explore.
Sorted by alphabet.

Arrival

Humanity is on the verge of global war and multiple stone-like spaceships land on Earth to make first contact.

Blade Runner 2049

30 years after the original events, LAPD officer K is on the journey to uncover a long buried secret.

Cloud Atlas

A massive scale story that tells the story of six timelines, ranging from 1849 to 2346.

Dawn

The second installment of the Planet of the Apes trilogy.

Interstellar

Humanity's time on Earth is coming to an end, so a team of exlporers travels beyond our neighbouring stars.

Logan

A young mutant starts a disrupting war about her future.

Midnight Special

A father does everything to protect his son and help fullfill his destiny.

Oblivion

One of the last drone repairmen on Earth triggers events that make him question everything he thought he knew.

Rise

The first installment of the Planet of the Apes trilogy.

Super 8

Six friends unintentionally film a train crash and discover something unbelievable.

The Ballad of Buster Scruggs

Six tales of life and violence in the Old West.

Tomorrowland

Bound by a shared destiny, two people embark on a mission to unearth secrets of a place that exists in their collective memory.

War

The third installment of the Planet of the Apes trilogy.
