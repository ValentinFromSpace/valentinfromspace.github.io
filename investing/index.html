Crypto Portfolio

  • Cardano (ADA)
    Cardano is a verifiably secure and decentralized proof-of-stake blockchain with native multi-asset and smart contract capabilities. The project has a strong focus on Africa.
  • Filecoin (FIL)
    Filecoin is a decentrlized storage network with its own proof-of-spacetime consensus mechanism. Development is led by Protocol Labs, the creators of IPFS and IPLD, the next-generation internet protocols.
  • Energy Web (EWT)
    Providing an energy operating system for decentralized energy grids to large partners, such as Shell. Energy is experiencing a transformative decade.
  • Ultra.io (UOS)
    Starting as a game store based on non-fungible tokens and incentives for buyers, developers and content creators, with plans to become an all-in-one entertainment platform connected through an underlying blockchain.
  • Chainlink (LINK)
    An oracle, providing data to smart contracts.

A larger stake in one particular asset over the other does not necessarily mean higher conviction but is subject to the rapid fluctuations in price endemic to crypto currencies.
