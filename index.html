IPFS!

The interplanetary file system, or IPFS, is the future of the internet. If you want to be part of that new internet, try out my website at ipfs.valwal.com. Keep in mind that my node may not be online at all times. IPFS is a decentralized peer-to-peer network, so you'll be communicating directly with my computer!

For the true Web 3 experience, download the Brave browser and enable its native IPFS integration. Brave will then download an IPFS node to your device and use the gateway of that local node. This means your device will be talking directly to my computer. Otherwise, your browser will get the website by connecting to a public gateway (in this case cloudflare-ipfs.com) via the regular old Web 2 tech stack.
